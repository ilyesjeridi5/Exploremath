✦ The Platform

What is
ExploreMath?

ExploreMath is a personal learning space built around curiosity. It brings together courses in mathematics, physics, and robotics — each one written to be clear, approachable, and genuinely useful. Whether you're working through foundational concepts or diving into hands-on robotics ideas, this platform is designed to make complex topics feel within reach. It's not just a collection of notes — it's a living space that grows with every new idea worth sharing.

✦ The Creator
👤

Ilyes Jeridi

Creator of ExploreMath

Ilyes is someone who finds genuine joy in understanding how things work — not just in theory, but all the way down to the equations, the circuits, and the logic behind them. His passion for mathematics and physics isn't something he keeps to himself; it's something he wants to share. Robotics, for him, is where those two worlds meet in the most exciting way possible. ExploreMath is his way of putting that energy out into the world — one lesson, one idea, one exercise at a time.
